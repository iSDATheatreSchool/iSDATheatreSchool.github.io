Brackley

Adventurers

For our youngest stars aged 2.5 - 6, we host magical sessions every Friday at Bracken Leas School in Brackley. After School and Lunch Clubs

Are you looking to add an engaging and dynamic after-school club to your school’s program in Northamptonshire or Warwickshire? Look no further! Our Performing Arts School brings a variety of exciting group classes directly to you, designed to inspire and delight students of all ages.

We offer a range of classes that cater to diverse interests:

  • Musical Theatre: Combine singing, dancing, and acting in a thrilling all-in-one experience.
  • Street Dance: Get groovy with energetic moves in this vibrant dance genre.
  • Ballet: Build the foundation of dancing with grace and technique.
  • Tap: Let your feet do the talking with rhythmic and percussive steps.
  • Jazz: Express yourself through the classic, upbeat style of jazz dance.
  • Drama: Discover your inner Shakespeare and explore the world of performance.
  • Singing: Unleash your inner star and let your voice shine.

Our after-school clubs are priced from just £6.50 per child, making it accessible for all. We ensure that every child has a fantastic time learning with our:

  • Professionally Trained Staff: Our instructors are skilled and experienced in their fields.
  • DBS Checked: Safety is a priority, and all staff are thoroughly vetted.
  • Friendly and Experienced: Our team is dedicated to creating a positive and supportive environment.

Our programs are designed to build confidence, enhance self-esteem, and develop specific skills in musical theatre, all while fostering teamwork and life-long skills. Make learning fun and join us in bringing a burst of creative energy to your school’s after-school activities!

Meet our expert Staff

Sophie March, PGCE Secondary Drama, Diploma in Professional Musical Theatre

Principal and Lead Instructor

Sophie's journey in the performing arts began at the tender age of 3 with ballet, sparking a lifelong passion that has only grown stronger over the years. With over 8 years of experience teaching children from newborns to 18-year-olds, Sophie brings a wealth of knowledge and enthusiasm to every lesson.

Sophie is a fully qualified secondary Drama teacher, having earned her PGCE, and her expertise extends beyond the classroom. As an active professional performer, she travels across the country, showcasing her talents in a variety of shows, theatres, events, and private functions. This ongoing involvement in the performing arts ensures that she brings the latest industry insights and trends into her teaching.

Her love for the performing arts is not only evident in her dynamic teaching but also in her commitment to sharing her up-to-date knowledge with the next generation. Sophie's unique blend of experience, passion, and professionalism makes her an inspiring mentor for all her students.

At iSing iDance iAct you can trust that your child is learning from a dedicated and experienced educator who is deeply invested in their success and growth in the performing arts.
